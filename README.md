## What I am exploring

- Small language models that are actually useful
- Fine-tuning and publishing models on Hugging Face
- Local and on-device AI
- MCP servers and agent tools
- Long-term memory systems for AI agents
- Mobile-first AI products
- Burmese AI tools and language technology

> I believe small, focused models will matter much more than people expect — especially when they are paired with good product design, memory, and real tools.

## How I think about building

I don't only care about making demos. I care about turning ideas into working products, testing them quickly, and learning from what breaks.

Some of my projects are serious.  
Some are playful.  
Some are experiments.

All of them are part of the same direction: building useful software with **AI and human context** at the center.
